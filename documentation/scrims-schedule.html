Scrims schedule

Explanation of the Scrims schedule settings category.

In all sections make sure to input time in CET (UTC+1) timezone. Use format Day HH:MM. More times are allowed, input each one on new line.

Notifications posting

At what time Notification message will be sent.

Registrations for TOP role

At what time TOP Registration open message will be sent and Registration channel for TOP tier role opened.

Registrations for Middle role

At what time Middle Registration open message will be sent and Registration channel for Middle tier role opened.

Registrations for Scrims access role

At what time Scrims access Registration open message will be sent and Registration channel for Scrims access role opened.

Closing registration

At what time will be Registration channel closed.

Confirmation opening

At what time will be Confirmation message sent and Confirmation started.

Start of first match

At what time will your scrims start. This is used in Scrims Finder to let players knew at what time first match starts.
