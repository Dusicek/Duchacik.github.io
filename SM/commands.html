/scrims

- Sends today's scrims. You can specify day and time.

/register {team_name} {team_tag}

- Register your team in scrims. You can asign another user to team manager.

/admin activate

- [ADMIN] Activates scrims on server.

/admin clear

- [ADMIN] Refreshes scrims season.

/admin close

- [ADMIN] Closes the registration.

/admin config

- [ADMIN] Scrims configuration.

/admin confirm

- [ADMIN] Starts scrims confirmation.

/admin custom

- [ADMIN] Sends custom message.

/admin deactivate

- [ADMIN] Deactivates scrims on server.

/admin link

- [ADMIN] Sends share link for results table.

/admin notify

- [ADMIN] Sends scrims notification message.

/admin ractive

- [ADMIN] Activates results for server.

/admin registration

- [ADMIN] Opens the registration channel.

/admin remove {lobby} {slots}

- [ADMIN] Removes team from specified lobby and slot.

/admin results {lobby}

- [ADMIN] Returns results of given lobby.

/admin sheet

- [ADMIN] Updates team list in sheets.
