Messages

Explanation of the Messages settings category > text of messages for events.

Notification

Message that will be sent in notification channel after using b notify command or in configured time in Scrims schedule settings category.

TOP Registration open

Message that will be sent in registration channel after opening registration by b register command or when registration for TOP tier role opens in configured time in Scrims schedule settings category.

Middle Registration open

Message that will be sent in registration channel after opening registration by b register command or when registration for Middle tier role opens in configured time in Scrims schedule settings category.

Scrims access Registraion open

Message that will be sent in registration channel after opening registration by b register command or when registration for Scrims access role opens in configured time in Scrims schedule settings category.

Registraion closed

Message that will be sent in registration channel after closing registration by b close command or when registration is closed in configured time in Scrims schedule settings category.

Slots confirmation

Message that will be sent in lobby channel for confirmation after using b confirm command or when confirmation is opened in configured time in Scrims schedule settings category.

Waitlist confirmation

Message that will be sent in waitlist channel for confirmation after using b confirm command or when confirmation is opened in configured time in Scrims schedule settings category.

Custom message

Custom message that will be sent by b custom command.
