Scrims schedule

Explanation of the Scrims schedule settings category.

In all sections make sure to input time in CET (UTC+1) or CEST (UTC+2) timezone depending on time of year. Use format Day HH:MM. More times are allowed, input each one on new line.

So if you want bot to perform action just one time:

Monday 16:00

If you want bot to perform same action in different times:

  • Monday 10:00
  • Monday 16:00

If you want bot to perform same action in different days:

  • Monday 16:00
  • Tuseday 16:00
  • Wednesday 16:00
  • Thursday 16:00

Closing registration

At what time will be Registration channel closed.

Confirmation opening

At what time will be Confirmation message sent and Confirmation started.

Start of first match

At what time will your scrims start. This is used in Scrims Finder to let players knew at what time first match starts.
