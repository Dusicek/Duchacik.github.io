Roles

Explanation of the Roles settings category.

Lobby

Role for Team managers.

Scrims access

Role for these who have access to Registration. Usually it is role for scrims section.

Announcement

Role that is given to user after %register. BUT this role is not removed after b clear command. You can use it for knowing how many active Team managers you have on server. If you dont want to do something special with this role, configure same role as Scrims access.

Waitlist

Role that is given to user after %register. He has it until he is assigned to lobby.

Ban

Role for banned Users (Team managers). If user have this role, he is unable to register trough %register command.

TOP Tier

Role for Top Tier (T1) Team managers. You can open your registration in different time then for Scrims access or Middle Tier.

Middle Tier

Role for Middle Tier (T2) Team managers. You can open your registration in different time then for Scrims access or TOP Tier.
