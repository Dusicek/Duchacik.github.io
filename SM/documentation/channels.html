Channels

Explanation of the Channels settings category.

Lobby

Channel that is for Managers of teams. Slots and Slots confirmation message is sent here. Usually announcements for Managers of teams and ID & PW is sent here too

Accept teams

Channel for Scrims admin where Scrims admins accept teams, change lobby of team (if you have more lobbies) or remove teams from slot. Make sure it is visible ONLY for Scrims admin/Scrims staff.

Notification

Channel for sending notification, about opening registration soon. Usually configured same channel as for Registration or as seperated channel for announcement for scrims.

Registration

Channel where Managers of teams can register their team with /register command

and this is channel where is messsage about opening the registration (or registrations if you have more tiers) sent.

Waitlist

Channel for Team managers with Waitlist role. Waitlist confirmation message is sent here.
