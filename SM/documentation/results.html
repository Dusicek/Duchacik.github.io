Results

Explanation of the Results & Results font colour section in General settings.

Results

Firstly you need to activate results with /admin ractive command. If you don't have them activated you will see this message: Not activated, activate them using command /admin ractive.

Then with /admin link command you will get link to your results Google sheet.. This is where you will write results of your scrims (Wins, kills, place).

If you dont see your teams in the sheet use /admin sheet command to update teams in Google sheet (you can't write team names manually in the sheet).

You can customize your points (for kills, place, wins) on the left side of Google sheet.

Custom results background

If you want to have Custom background for your results you need to have image (PNG, JPEG or WEBP) in the size equal to the number of slots. Here are some dimensions:

  • 10 slots - 1920px x 840px
  • 12 slots - 1920px x 920px
  • 14 slots - 1920px x 1000px
  • 16 slots - 1920px x 1080px
  • 18 slots - 1920px x 1160px
  • 20 slots - 1920px x 1240px
  • 22 slots - 1920px x 1320px
  • 24 slots - 1920px x 1400px
