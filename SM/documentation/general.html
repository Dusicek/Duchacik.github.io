General

Explanation of the General settings category, but without Results section, that has seperated page.

Name of scrims

Name of scrims that will be visible in Scrims Finder and on Results message.

Scrims admin

Role for Scrims admins/Scrims staff. Bot accepts ADMIN commands only from users with this role.

Scrims in Scrims Finder

Choose if you want to have your scrims visible in Scrims Finder/if you want to have them publicly accesible, through /scrims command.

If your scrims are public, you will see this message: Scrims are public

If your scrims are not public, you will see this message: Scrims are not listed in Scrims Finder

Lobbies

Number of lobbies.

Slots

Number of slots in lobby/lobbies.

First slot

First slots. It is useful when you don't want players to jump in someone elses slots when joining lobby in game.
